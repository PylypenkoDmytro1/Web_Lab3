The Gods

The world of Fear & Hunger lacks a unified religion, instead featuring powerful deities known as the Old Gods, worshipped by various factions. These gods embody primordial concepts and inspirations, with their nature partially revealed through in-game documents. While only six (or five, depending on interpretations of Alll-mer) deities are known, ten runic symbols have been depicted, possibly indicating their total number or representing those known to civilization.

The series hints that some Old Gods originate from a mysterious "green hue," a void-like plane with a green-tinted reality that discomforts human eyes. This hue may act as a portal between planes, as servants of these deities are seen emerging from it.

Lore suggests some Old Gods have "departed" but left behind sentient, influential traces. These deities may never fully vanish, as their essence represents unextinguishable ideas and concepts. However, belief in their permanence is not universally shared.
